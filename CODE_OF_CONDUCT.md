# Code Of Conduct

AnyBridge follows a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and technical.
- Assume good intent, but keep discussions evidence-based.
- Do not post secrets, account data, captured traffic, or private user information.
- Keep issue threads focused on reproducible behavior and actionable proposals.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that is abusive, spammy, illegal, or unsafe to publish.
