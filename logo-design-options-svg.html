AnyBridge SVG Logo Options

Second round: ten logo directions drawn as inline SVG, closer to something that can be copied into the app later. Each direction is tested on light and dark topbars.

01 Node Bridge
network mark
Most literal: four endpoints connected by a bridge path. Good if the brand should communicate routing immediately.
02 AB Monogram
compact
A more product-like app mark. Useful if you eventually want a taskbar/app icon variant.
03 Relay Arc
motion implied
Bridge as a relay arc. Cleaner and friendlier than a dense network icon.
04 Route Glyph
minimal icon
A routed path with arrow-like terminals. Very compact and tool-like.
05 Split Panels
console
Two configuration panes bridged together. Reads well at small sizes.
06 Hex Link
system
More infrastructural. Good if you want the app to feel like a control-plane tool.
07 Terminal Bridge
developer
Developer-tool flavor. Strong in dark mode, slightly heavier in the top-left.
08 Ribbon Crossing
friendly
Soft crossing ribbons. More playful and memorable than the stricter system marks.
09 Signal Stack
fast
Horizontal signal lanes. Reads as speed, mapping, and multi-provider routing.
10 Plain Premium
wordmark
The most conservative option: no icon, just a small structural accent. Very safe for a dense admin UI.
